About Release

Sometimes, leading the hectic life of a college student can get in the way of our self-care routines. Need some time to revive yourself? Then welcome to "Release". This is a website dedicated to helping Howard University students manage their moods! Feel free to LOG how you feel, CREATE journal entries, INTERACT with our suggestions, and POST to our chatroom.
